The Weekend

We are thrilled to be hosting a Revision Satellite this year. Join us in Limehouse, London, to watch the stream on a big screen with a loud sound system, and most importantly, other sceners!

This is a hobby project, we’re just renting a venue, projector and sound system for our friends in the UK demoscene to get together and take part in the Revision Demoparty. Check out the main site here.

Anyone who is involved in or interested in the demoscene is welcome. We would love to see you there.

The Venue

The venue is Limehouse Town Hall, 646 Commercial Road, London. We have a maximum capacity of 25 people.

It is closed overnight. You will have to make your own sleeping arrangements. We couldn’t find a venue that permitted traditional demoscene sleeping bags under tables.

Staying Safe

We expect you to take an LFT each day and stay home if you are infected. Wear a mask when you are indoors or around other people.

Bigotry, harassment, verbal or physical abuse, and anything intended to harm other people at the party is unwelcome and may result in you being barred entry.

Don’t let random strangers into the building during the event.

The Cost

Reality404 has hired a hall for the weekend, which costs quite a bit of money. We’re asking for an entrance fee of £40 if you can afford it, if you can’t then just pay what you can. We’ll provide a projector, sound system, and maybe some revision goodies. You have to provide the rest.
