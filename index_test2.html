About

Field-FX - Silvester 31.12.2021 to 01.01.2022

What's Silvester?

It's an online New Year's party for the demoscene. People will make demos, music, and art, and we're going to show them at the party, on New Year's Eve. We hope you will join us, and maybe even make something! The party itself will be hosted on our Field-FX Twitch stream, so if you are missing out on the partying this year, and you want to hang out with the lovely people of the demoscene, come and join us!

Is this what Field-FX is about?>

No! Field-FX is a demoparty in a field! The next one will be at EMF Camp 2022, and we are hoping that it can be in person. But right now our winter partying plans have been disrupted, and we want to kick out the jams!

If you've never encountered the demoscene before, the wikipedia article is a good introduction https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Demoscene

Rules

* Please include a screenshot and video capture of your intro in 1280x720 MP4 format.

* For executable entries in the 128 byte, 256 byte and 512 byte categories, we will look at the binary filesize (including any headers).

* For executable entries of 64 bytes or less, we will not count the header size (given that the header is not larger than the intro itself)

* Our definition of oldschool platforms is: 8/16 bit home computers, 8/16 bit game consoles and handhelds and 8088 PC's (8 mhz, MDA/CGA/EGA)

* Our definition of high-end platforms is: X86/DOS (386+, VGA), Linux, Windows, RISCOS, Motorola 68040+, and Fantasy Consoles/VM.

* All entries will have a running time of maximum 1 minute, unless specifically requested by the author.

* You can enter as many competitions you like, with as many productions as you like.

* Only original and unreleased works are allowed.

* Please include a FILE_ID.DIZ with a description of your prod.

* The organizers' decisions are final. We want to show off all your work to the standard it deserves, but we are doing this in our spare time and things can go wrong. Getting your entries in before the deadline is a good way to ensure that it gets shown!
