Field-FXmas 2024 - A demoscene stream and showcase on Twitch

Monday 30th December 2024

As we say goodbye to 2024, we would like to invite you all to join us for our apparently annual end-of-year Field-FX stream to celebrate the past year of the demoscene, to cheer you all with fresh demos, creativity and DJ sets, to celebrate our beloved scene family, and to have a good time in the chat. We would like you to feel welcome even if you're not yet a part of the scene, but have a curious or creative mind, so please do join us and have a good time with us on Twitch on the 30th of December.

If you have time and would like to make something, we are accepting demos, animations, art and music for a final end of year showcase.

Whatever your plans, we hope 2024 has been an inspiring year for you, and that you are ready to step into 2025 with positive demoscene spirit in your heart and mind.

Code of conduct

We are committed to making Field FX an inclusive and welcoming event. Unfortunately, sometimes harassment happens. While we are present on site at EMF Camp we are all subject to the EMF Camp Code of Conduct and our organisers are invested in supporting it.

We will also be extending the same Code of Conduct to our party stream. Moderators will be present in online channels operated by Field FX and their identity will be established by a group message at regular intervals throughout the stream. Tagging them in your message will elicit a response. During a stream we aim to reach a decision on all conduct issues within 10 minutes. Outside of channels operated by us we cannot guarantee support. All reports will be taken seriously and will be considered in confidence by our team.
