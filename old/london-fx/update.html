Update for visitors

Dear scener,

With just a few days to go until Revision, we thought we’d drop all our attendees a letter to let you know some final details.

Our hall hire is being kindly covered on a donation basis. Limehouse Town Hall is one of the only open spaces of its type in London, we want to keep that happening and enable them to run more cool events and projects. Because of this, we are asking attendees to make a donation towards the event, to help cover some of our costs, and give a significant donation to the hall. We suggest a donation of £30, but you are free to give more or less depending on your circumstances, details TBC for how to donate but we’ll let you know when it’s figured out.

When you get to Limehouse Town Hall, ring the doorbell that says Boxing Club and wait for someone to answer. We’ll check you off the list of registered attendees and give you an official Revision wristband for the weekend. Blåhajs are welcome. There is wifi, power, and beer fridges in the hall. We are going to be in the hall from...

  • 14:00 - 01:00 on Friday
  • 18:00 - 01:00 on Saturday
  • 09:30 - 01:00 on Sunday
  • 09:30 - prizegiving and tidyup

The Revision stream starts at 15:00 on Friday, 10:00 every other day. We are looking at the options for how to enjoy the afternoon compos on Saturday, if you have any ideas let us know! The hall will be open till 1am.

A few of us will be partaking in the famous Revision 5k run on Saturday morning. You are welcome to join us running, walking, or wobbling between local pubs. There is a Strava if you’re keen, more details here: https://2022.revision-party.net/events/special-events/

If you feel like hanging around on Monday after the prizegiving to help tidy up, that will be very welcome. And if you need to get in touch with the orgas for any reason, give us a ring!

  • Aldroid (Alex): +44 7855 279995
  • reality404 (Reality): +44 7760 887933
  • Crypt (Martin) +44 7913 053358

Read through the main site on https://field-fx.party/london-fx before you leave, to make sure you haven’t missed anything.

We’re really excited to being able to share Revision with sceners in real life once more, and looking forward to seeing you there!

– Aldroid, Crypt and Reality
